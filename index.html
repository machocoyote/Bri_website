Our Story

Floral artistry
from the heart

Be Flourished was born from a deep love of flowers and a passion for turning life's most meaningful moments into unforgettable memories. Every stem is chosen with intention, every arrangement composed to tell your unique story.

Based in Cleveland, Ohio, we specialize in lush, garden-inspired designs — from intimate hand-tied bouquets to grand wedding installations. No order is too small; every bloom deserves the same devotion.
